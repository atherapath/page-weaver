Loneliness Poem

Homeless on the streets alone and watching time go by, falling deeper into loneliness, not matter what I try.

Catagorised as crazy and sectioned of as strange, the intimacy I crave inside is always out of range.

They tell you how to live your life, they tell you how to think, and if you pull yourself together you can even have a drink.

But what if I don't like to drink and like the way I currently think?

I like peice and quiet, retreating to the birds, following in the glow of trees and leaving the obserds.

The feeling of together without a word spoken, the recognition that something here is maybe just a bit broken.

The tending to my loneliness, in the everlasting days.
